# **App Name**: Peritar Online

## Core Features:

- Homepage: Display general information about Peritar, including the company's mission, vision, and values, as well as the logos of registered insurance companies. Implement an authentication button.
- About Us Page: Detail the company's background, mission, vision, and objectives.
- Services Page: Showcase the services offered by Peritar.
- Contact Page: Provide a contact form and contact information for users to reach Peritar.
- Role-Based Dashboards: Implement dedicated dashboards for different roles (Super Usuario, Aseguradora, Talleres, Peritos, and Clientes) with customized functionalities.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#30475E), reflecting trust and professionalism, based on the logo's color.
- Background color: Light gray (#E0E5EC), for a clean and modern look, while retaining some of the darkness from the logo color.
- Accent color: Sky blue (#76B4EB), mirroring the sky blue found in the provided logo to highlight key elements.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) to provide a modern, friendly look and maintain readability across the different types of content on the site.
- Use professional, clear icons that complement the services offered and the roles within the platform.
- Maintain a clean and structured layout to facilitate easy navigation and access to information for all users.
- Implement subtle animations and transitions to improve user experience and provide visual feedback on interactions.
